Key Technical Insights
* Centralized Data Pipeline: To ensure sustainability, the library aims to create a semi-automated model for annual updates. This includes a standardized method for cleaning and normalizing data from disparate sources like EZproxy and Moodle.
* AI Interpretation: The AI Insights Bot is trained on the library's specific knowledge base to interpret raw numbers from these repositories (e.g., explaining why a 15% increase in instructional interactions matters for PNWU Goal 1).
* Infrastructure Sustainability: The library is transitioning its internal documentation and handbook to a structured SharePoint repository to ensure that the staff responsible for these metrics have a "go-to resource" for maintenance workflows.

Sample questions to ask the dashboard AI:


Students (Focus: Affordability, academic support, and peer success narratives.)
* How much money have I collectively saved through the library's Course Materials Initiative this year?
* Is it worth my time to use the 'Book a Librarian' service?  What can I expect?
* How does the Clinical Pathways course in Moodle help me in my future clinical career?
* Are other students satisfied with the library’s resources?
* What is the most popular resources consulted for Anatomy?


Faculty (Focus: Curriculum alignment, resource costs, and student outcomes.)
* What are the top three most-used resources for my specific academic program right now?
* How many students reached the 80% competency benchmark in the information literacy modules for my course?
* What is the cost difference between an article license for my classroom versus the library fulfilling a request through Interlibrary Loan?
* What datasets can I use for my personal research on ….?


Administration/Staff (Focus: Operational efficiency, workflow improvement, and service visibility.)
* What is the trend for self-service activity (like 24/7 chat) compared to manual reference appointments?
* Are students feeling more confident about finding authoritative health information after using the library's Moodle modules?
* Can you explain the trend in how often students are using the 24/7 chat reference compared to last year?
* How does the library specifically support my preparation for standardized licensure exams?
